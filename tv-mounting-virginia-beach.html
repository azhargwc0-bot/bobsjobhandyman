Professional TV Mounting Installation Process

Common Questions

TV Mounting FAQ

Common questions about our professional TV mounting services in Virginia Beach, answered by our certified installation specialists.

Can you mount my TV the same day I call?

+

Yes! We offer same-day TV mounting services in Virginia Beach for most installations. Our team keeps common mount types and hardware in stock, allowing us to complete most TV mounting jobs within hours of your call. Contact us early in the day for best same-day availability.

What's the largest TV size you can mount?

+

We mount TVs from 32 inches up to 85 inches and larger. Large TV installations require specialized heavy-duty mounts and proper wall reinforcement. Our Virginia Beach team has experience with all TV sizes and can handle even custom oversized displays safely and securely.

Can you hide all the cables and wires?

+

Absolutely! Professional cable management is included with all our TV mounting services. We can route cables through walls, install decorative cord covers, or add new outlets behind your TV. Every installation includes wire management to maintain a clean, professional appearance.

What wall types can you mount TVs on?

+

Our Virginia Beach team mounts TVs on all wall types: drywall with wood studs, metal studs, brick, concrete, cinder block, and plaster walls. We use appropriate anchors and mounting hardware for each wall type to ensure maximum security and safety for your TV installation.

How much does professional TV mounting cost?

+

TV mounting costs vary based on TV size, mount type, wall complexity, and cable management needs. Basic installations start at competitive rates, while complex full-motion mounts with extensive wire hiding cost more. We provide upfront pricing with no hidden fees before starting any work.

Do you provide the TV mount or do I need to buy it?

+

We can provide professional-grade TV mounts as part of our service, or install mounts you've already purchased. Our team stocks high-quality fixed, tilting, and full-motion mounts for all TV sizes. We'll recommend the best mount type for your specific needs and viewing preferences.

How high should my TV be mounted on the wall?

+

Proper TV height depends on your seating arrangement and TV size. Generally, the center of your TV screen should be at eye level when seated. Our Virginia Beach team evaluates your room layout and seating to determine optimal mounting height for comfortable viewing without neck strain.

Can you mount TVs above fireplaces?

+

Yes, we can mount TVs above fireplaces with proper heat considerations and safety measures. We use heat-resistant mounts, ensure adequate clearance, and may recommend tilting mounts for better viewing angles. Virginia Beach fireplace installations require special attention to both heat and viewing comfort.

What if I need to move my mounted TV later?

+

We can relocate your mounted TV to a different wall or room. TV remounting services include safe removal, wall repair at the original location, and professional reinstallation at the new location. We'll patch and touch up paint at the original mounting point for a clean finish.

Do you work in condos and apartments?

+

Yes! We provide TV mounting services throughout Virginia Beach including condos, apartments, and rental properties. We're experienced with Oceanfront condos and can work within property management guidelines. We'll discuss any rental restrictions and provide solutions that meet your needs.

Is professional TV mounting worth the cost?

+

Professional TV mounting ensures safety, proper placement, and clean cable management that DIY installations often lack. We prevent wall damage, ensure secure mounting, and provide warranties on our work. The peace of mind and professional results make our TV mounting services a smart investment for your Virginia Beach home.

How long does a typical TV mounting take?

+

Most TV mounting installations take 1-2 hours depending on complexity. Simple fixed mount installations on drywall are quicker, while full-motion mounts with extensive cable management take longer. We provide time estimates upfront and work efficiently to complete your installation promptly.
