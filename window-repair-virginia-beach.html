Common Questions

Window Repair FAQ

Frequently asked questions about window repair services in Virginia Beach, answered by our experienced coastal property specialists.

How does Virginia Beach's coastal climate affect windows?

+

Virginia Beach's salt air accelerates hardware corrosion, high humidity causes wood frame swelling, and storms create physical damage. UV exposure degrades seals and weatherstripping faster than inland areas. Regular maintenance and quality repairs using coastal-rated materials extend window life significantly.

Can you repair windows damaged by hurricanes?

+

Yes, we specialize in hurricane damage restoration including broken glass, damaged frames, and destroyed screens. We provide emergency board-up services, insurance documentation, and complete restoration. Our storm damage repairs often include upgrades for better future protection.

What causes windows to stick and become difficult to operate?

+

Virginia Beach's humidity causes wood frames to swell, paint buildup in tracks, and hardware corrosion. Broken sash cords, failed balance systems, and warped frames also prevent smooth operation. We restore proper function through cleaning, lubrication, and hardware replacement.

Do broken window seals really affect energy efficiency?

+

Absolutely. Failed seals allow conditioned air to escape and humid air to enter, significantly increasing energy costs. Virginia Beach's climate makes this worse due to constant temperature and humidity differences. Proper sealing and weatherstripping repairs provide immediate energy savings.

Is it worth repairing old windows or should I replace them?

+

Well-maintained older windows can often be restored for much less than replacement cost. We assess window condition, energy efficiency potential, and repair costs to help you make informed decisions. Quality repairs can extend window life 10-20 years in Virginia Beach conditions.

What safety glass requirements apply in Virginia Beach?

+

Safety glass is required in doors, sidelites, windows near stairs, large windows close to floors, and shower/bath areas. Virginia Beach follows standard building codes for safety glass placement. We ensure all glass replacements meet current safety requirements.

Can you match glass in older windows?

+

We can match most older glass types including single-pane, wavy glass, and specialty glazing. For unique or antique glass, we work with specialty suppliers to find closest matches. When exact matches aren't available, we recommend alternatives that maintain the window's character.

How long do window screen repairs typically last?

+

Quality screen repairs using marine-grade materials last 5-10 years in Virginia Beach conditions. We use corrosion-resistant frames and durable screening mesh designed for coastal environments. Regular cleaning and gentle handling extend screen life significantly.

Do you provide emergency window repair services?

+

Yes, we provide emergency services for broken windows that compromise security or weather protection. This includes storm damage, break-in damage, and accidental breakage. We offer temporary board-up services and prioritize permanent repairs to restore security and comfort.

What's the best way to maintain windows in Virginia Beach?

+

Regular cleaning to remove salt deposits, annual inspection of seals and weatherstripping, lubrication of hardware, and prompt repair of minor issues prevent major problems. We recommend professional inspections every 2-3 years in Virginia Beach's challenging coastal environment.

Can window repairs improve home security?

+

Yes, we can install security hardware, reinforce frames, upgrade locks, and install security film or laminated glass. These improvements enhance security while maintaining window functionality. Many security upgrades also provide additional storm protection benefits.

Do you work with insurance companies for storm damage claims?

+

Yes, we work with insurance companies regularly for Virginia Beach storm damage claims. We provide detailed documentation, damage assessments, and repair estimates. Our experience with coastal storm damage helps ensure proper claim processing and complete restoration coverage.
