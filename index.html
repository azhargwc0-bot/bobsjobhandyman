Common Questions

Get answers to the most frequently asked questions about our handyman services.

How quickly can you respond to emergency repairs?

+

For true emergencies like burst pipes or electrical hazards, we typically arrive within 2-4 hours. For urgent but non-dangerous issues, same-day service is usually available. We understand that some problems can't wait, especially when they affect your family's safety or comfort.

Do you provide free estimates?

+

Yes, we provide free estimates for all non-emergency work. For smaller jobs under $200, we can often give you an accurate quote over the phone. For larger projects, we'll schedule a convenient time to visit your home and provide a detailed written estimate at no charge.

What areas of Virginia Beach do you serve?

+

We serve all of Virginia Beach, including Oceanfront, Hilltop, Kempsville, Princess Anne, and surrounding neighborhoods. Our central location allows us to respond quickly throughout the city. We also serve nearby areas in Chesapeake and Norfolk when scheduling permits.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Absolutely. We're fully licensed as a home improvement contractor in Virginia, bonded, and carry comprehensive liability insurance. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request. This protects both you and us in the unlikely event something goes wrong.
