Fence Repair FAQ

Fence Repair Questions

Common questions about professional fence repair services in Virginia Beach.

What types of fence damage can you repair?

+

We repair all types of fence damage including broken boards, leaning posts, sagging gates, storm damage, rot, cracked vinyl panels, bent chain link fabric, damaged hardware, and structural issues. We work with wood, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, and composite fencing materials.

How do you determine if a fence should be repaired or replaced?

+

We evaluate the extent of damage, age of the fence, overall condition, and cost comparison. Generally, if repair costs exceed 50-60% of replacement cost, or if the fence has widespread structural issues, replacement may be more economical. We provide honest assessments to help you decide.

Can you match existing fence materials and colors?

+

Yes, we specialize in matching existing fence materials, wood types, stain colors, and vinyl shades. We source matching materials and can custom-stain wood to blend seamlessly with your existing fence for repairs that are virtually invisible.

Do you provide emergency fence repair after storms?

+

Yes, we offer emergency fence repair services after hurricanes, severe storms, and other damage events. We can provide temporary securing to protect your property and schedule permanent repairs as quickly as possible to restore security and privacy.

How long do fence repairs typically take?

+

Repair time depends on damage extent and fence type. Simple board replacement takes 1-2 hours. Gate repairs typically require 2-4 hours. Extensive storm damage repairs may take 1-3 days. We provide accurate time estimates and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Can you repair gates that are sagging or won't close properly?

+

Absolutely! Gate problems are common and highly repairable. We fix sagging gates by adjusting hinges, replacing hardware, adding support braces, or realigning posts. We also repair latches, locks, and automatic gate openers for smooth, secure operation.

What causes fence posts to lean or become loose?

+

Post problems are usually caused by wind damage, soil erosion, inadequate concrete footings, or freeze-thaw cycles. In Virginia Beach, high winds and sandy soil are common culprits. We straighten posts, add proper concrete footings, and use wind-resistant installation techniques.

Do you work with insurance companies for storm damage claims?

+

Yes, we regularly work with insurance companies and provide documentation needed for storm damage claims. We can photograph damage, provide detailed estimates, and work within insurance guidelines to restore your fence to pre-storm condition.

How do coastal conditions affect different fence materials?

+

Salt air accelerates corrosion of metal components and can damage wood finishes. High humidity promotes rot in untreated wood. Strong winds stress posts and panels. We use corrosion-resistant hardware, proper sealers, and wind-resistant techniques for coastal durability.

Can you repair vinyl fence cracks and breaks?

+

Yes, we repair vinyl fence damage including cracks, breaks, and impact damage. Depending on severity, we can patch small cracks or replace entire panels. We stock common vinyl fence colors and styles for seamless repairs that match your existing fence.

Do you offer preventive maintenance programs?

+

Yes, we offer preventive maintenance programs including annual inspections, cleaning, staining, hardware tightening, and minor repairs. Regular maintenance extends fence life significantly and prevents small problems from becoming major repairs.

What's your warranty on fence repair work?

+

We provide a satisfaction guarantee on all repair work and warranty our workmanship. Specific warranty terms depend on the repair type and materials used. We stand behind our work and will return to address any issues with our repairs at no additional charge.
