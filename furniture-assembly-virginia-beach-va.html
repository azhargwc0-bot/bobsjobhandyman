Assembly FAQ

Furniture Assembly Questions

Common questions about our furniture assembly service in Virginia Beach VA.

How much does furniture assembly cost in Virginia Beach?

+

Furniture assembly costs vary by piece complexity. Simple items like nightstands or small tables typically cost $50-100, while complex pieces like entertainment centers or wardrobes range from $150-300. We provide upfront pricing before starting work.

Do you assemble IKEA kitchen cabinets?

+

Yes, we specialize in IKEA kitchen cabinet assembly including the SEKTION system. Kitchen cabinet assembly is more complex and typically requires 1-2 days depending on kitchen size. We ensure proper alignment, secure mounting, and smooth door/drawer operation.

What if hardware or parts are missing from my furniture?

+

We carry common furniture hardware and can often substitute missing pieces with equivalent or better quality hardware. For specialty pieces, we'll help you contact the manufacturer for replacement parts and can return to complete assembly once parts arrive.

How long does furniture assembly typically take?

+

Assembly time depends on furniture complexity. Simple pieces (nightstands, small tables) take 30-60 minutes. Medium complexity items (dressers, desks) take 1-2 hours. Complex pieces (entertainment centers, wardrobes) take 2-4 hours. We provide time estimates with quotes.
