Drywall FAQ

Drywall Repair Questions

Common questions about our drywall repair service in Virginia Beach VA.

How much does drywall repair cost in Virginia Beach?

+

Drywall repair costs vary by damage size. Small holes (nail/screw holes) typically cost $75-150. Medium holes (doorknob size) range from $150-300. Large repairs or multiple holes range from $300-600. We provide upfront pricing before starting work.

Can you match any wall texture perfectly?

+

Yes, our drywall specialists have years of experience matching all texture types including orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, and custom textures. We use professional spraying and hand-texturing techniques to ensure invisible repairs that blend seamlessly with your existing walls.

How long does drywall repair take to complete?

+

Small repairs can often be completed in one visit (2-4 hours including drying time). Larger repairs may require 1-2 days to allow for proper drying between coats. We use fast-drying compounds when possible to minimize completion time while maintaining quality.

Do you handle lead-safe drywall repair in older Virginia Beach homes?

+

Yes, we're certified in lead-safe work practices for homes built before 1978. We follow EPA RRP (Renovation, Repair, Painting) guidelines to safely handle any lead paint that may be present, protecting both your family and our workers during the repair process.
