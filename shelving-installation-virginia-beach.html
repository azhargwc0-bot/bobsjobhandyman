Shelving FAQ

Shelving Installation Questions

Common questions about professional shelving installation services in Virginia Beach.

What types of shelving can you install?

+

We install all types of shelving including floating shelves, bracket shelves, built-in shelving, closet organization systems, garage storage, wire shelving, wood shelving, metal shelving, glass shelves, and corner shelving units. Custom solutions available for unique spaces.

How much weight can wall-mounted shelves hold?

+

Weight capacity depends on wall type, mounting method, and shelf construction. Properly mounted shelves in studs can hold 50-100+ lbs per shelf. We calculate exact weight limits for each installation and provide written capacity ratings for safety.

Can you install shelves on any wall type?

+

Yes, we install on drywall, plaster, brick, concrete, tile, and other wall surfaces. Each requires specific mounting hardware and techniques. We assess your walls and select appropriate anchors, screws, and installation methods for secure mounting.

Do you provide the shelving materials or just installation?

+

We offer both options. We can install shelves you've purchased, or we can source and provide materials as part of our service. We'll recommend quality products that fit your budget and style preferences for optimal results.

How long does shelving installation typically take?

+

Simple floating shelves take 1-2 hours. Closet organization systems require 3-6 hours. Built-in shelving can take a full day or more depending on complexity. We provide accurate time estimates during consultation and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Can you design custom shelving solutions?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in custom shelving solutions for unique spaces and specific needs. This includes built-ins, corner units, angled shelves, integrated lighting, and specialty storage. We'll design solutions that maximize your space efficiently.

What's the difference between floating shelves and bracket shelves?

+

Floating shelves use hidden brackets inside the shelf for a clean, minimalist look. Bracket shelves have visible supports underneath. Floating shelves offer cleaner aesthetics but may have lower weight capacity. We'll help you choose based on your needs and preferences.

Can shelving be installed in bathrooms and kitchens?

+

Yes, we install moisture-resistant shelving in bathrooms and kitchens. We use appropriate materials and finishes that withstand humidity and temperature changes. Special consideration given to areas near sinks, showers, and cooking areas.

Do you install closet organization systems?

+

Yes, we install complete closet organization systems including wire shelving, wood shelving, hanging rods, shoe racks, and accessory storage. We work with both reach-in and walk-in closets to maximize storage and improve organization.

What about garage and basement storage shelving?

+

We install heavy-duty shelving systems for garages and basements using moisture-resistant materials. Options include wall-mounted and freestanding units with high weight capacity for tools, seasonal items, and bulk storage. Adjustable configurations available.

How do you ensure shelves are level and secure?

+

We use professional levels, stud finders, and measuring tools for precise installation. All mounting points are checked for security, and shelves are tested for stability before completion. We follow industry standards for safe, professional results.

What's your warranty on shelving installation?

+

We provide a satisfaction guarantee on all installation work. If mounting hardware fails due to installation issues, we'll return to fix it at no charge. We stand behind our workmanship and use quality materials for long-lasting results.
